Quartz Powder

We manufacture best quality silica (Quartz) powder meeting all quality specifications for finest product. We are providing silica (Quartz) powder in different mesh sizes and as per industry requirement.

Quartz crystal is used extensively in the electronics sector due to its piezoelectric capabilities. It is also used to manufacture glass and labware, silica brick, cement, and mortar because it is a key component of sand. Sandblasting, glass grinding, and stonecutting all use ground quartz as an abrasive. Porcelain, scouring soaps, sandpaper, and wood fillers are all made with quartz powder. Solar panels have been used recently.
